Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is HomeIPTV?

HomeIPTV is an IPTV player that lets you watch your IPTV channels on iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple TV. It supports both Xtream Codes API and M3U playlist formats.

Q: Does HomeIPTV provide TV channels?

No. HomeIPTV is a player only. You need your own IPTV subscription from a service provider. The app allows you to connect to your existing IPTV service.

Q: How do I set up the app?

Open the app and choose your connection type:

  • Xtream Codes: Enter your server URL, username, and password provided by your IPTV service
  • M3U Playlist: Enter the URL of your M3U playlist file
Q: How do I switch channels while watching?
  • Apple TV: Use the Siri Remote's left/right swipe to change channels, up/down for volume
  • Mac: Use arrow keys (left/right for channels, up/down for volume, ESC to close)
  • iPhone/iPad: Use the on-screen controls
Q: How do I manage favorite channels?

Tap the "Edit" button on the channel list to open the favorites picker. Select the channels you want as favorites and tap "Done".

Q: A channel is not playing. What should I do?
  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify your IPTV subscription is active
  • Try a different channel to see if the issue is specific to one channel
  • Re-enter your credentials in Settings
Q: Is my data secure?

Yes. Your credentials are stored securely in Apple's Keychain on your device. No data is sent to our servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.
