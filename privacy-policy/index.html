Privacy Policy

HomeIPTV

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Summary: HomeIPTV does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your information stays on your device.

1. Introduction

HomeIPTV ("the App") is an IPTV player application available on iOS, macOS, and Apple TV. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The App is designed to function without collecting any personal data from its users.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not use cookies or similar technologies

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Connection credentials: Your IPTV server URL, username, and password (or M3U playlist URL) are stored securely in the device's Keychain
  • Favorite channels: Your selected favorite channels are stored locally using UserDefaults

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party. You can delete this data at any time by removing your account from the App or by deleting the App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App connects to IPTV servers that you provide. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party IPTV services. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any IPTV service provider you use.

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

5. Network Communications

The App communicates over the network solely to:

  • Connect to IPTV servers you have configured to retrieve channel lists and stream content
  • Load channel logos from URLs provided by your IPTV service
  • Fetch EPG (Electronic Program Guide) data from your IPTV service

All network communications are initiated by you and directed to servers of your choosing. We do not operate any servers and do not receive any of this data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the App does not collect any personal data from any user, it is compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar regulations.

7. Data Security

Your IPTV credentials are stored using Apple's Keychain Services, which provides hardware-level encryption. No data is transmitted to external servers operated by us.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data for us to provide, modify, or delete. All data stored by the App is on your device and under your control. You can:

  • Delete your stored credentials through the App's settings
  • Remove all App data by deleting the App from your device

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
